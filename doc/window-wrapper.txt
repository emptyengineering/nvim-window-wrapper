*window-wrapper.txt* Window Wrapper Plugin
========================================

A Neovim plugin that wraps the window selection around.

Usage
-----

To use:
> <C-w>h will wrap around to the right side of the screen if on the first window.
> <C-w>l will wrap around to the left side of the screen if on the last window.
